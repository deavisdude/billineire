# Cultural Authenticity Review Checklist

**Purpose**: Ensure accurate and research-informed representation of cultures  
**Constitution Reference**: Principle V - Cultural Authenticity & Ethics  
**Applies to**: All culture packs (initial and community-contributed)

---

  - Sources cited: _______________________________________________
  
- [ ] **Multiple Perspectives**: Research includes multiple sources/perspectives to avoid bias

---

## Content Review

- [ ] **Structure Designs**: Buildings reflect actual architectural styles and materials
  - Structures reviewed: __________________________________________
  
- [ ] **Profession Names**: Occupations are historically accurate and interesting
  - Professions reviewed: _________________________________________
  
- [ ] **Trade Items**: Items and trades reflect actual economic practices
  
- [ ] **Visual Style**: Textures and aesthetics align with cultural aesthetic traditions
  
- [ ] **Localization Keys**: Text is translatable and avoids culturally-specific idioms

---

## Balance & Gameplay

- [ ] **No Cultural Hierarchy**: Culture is not mechanically superior/inferior to others
  
- [ ] **Fair Representation**: Gameplay features do not imply cultural value judgments
  
- [ ] **Unique Identity**: Culture has distinct identity without relying on stereotypes

---

## Opt-Out & Override

- [ ] **Override Mechanism**: Server admins can disable or override this culture pack
  
- [ ] **Localization Support**: All user-facing text has localization keys for translation
  
- [ ] **Documentation**: Culture pack includes README with context, sources, and design notes
